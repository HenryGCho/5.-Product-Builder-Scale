The Product OS
Dimension Map

The operating system maps 16 capability dimensions across 3 phases. As agents increasingly do or support delivery, the human layer concentrates around judgment, framing, and taste — not execution.

A Dimensions marked agent-native are new or significantly changed in the context of agent-powered delivery — these are the emerging edges of founder capability.
01 · Understand
Get genuinely close to the customer, the market, and the problem before building anything.
02 · Envision
Define what to build, why it matters, and how agents and humans each play a role.
03 · Deliver, Run & Learn
Ship, operate, and close feedback loops — orchestrating agents and people to improve continuously.

Agent-native dimension
Key shift · Understand
The depth of your Understand work sets the ceiling for everything else. Agents amplify what they're given — so shallow understanding doesn't just slow you down, it compounds across every output.
Key shift · Envision
Agents work directly from your direction. A clear, specific vision produces clear, specific output at speed. Fuzzy direction produces fuzzy results — faster.
Key shift · Deliver
As agents handle more execution, the founder's role becomes directing rather than doing. The primary differentiator becomes human judgment — the final call on what's actually good enough to ship.
